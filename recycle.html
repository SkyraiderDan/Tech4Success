Electronics Recycling Program

Tech 4 Success provides a free drop off service for your electronics recycling needs at our Broad Street location. Our recycling process helps to ensure that your old electronics are properly disposed of so that they may become raw material for a device you may purchase in the future. We also refurbish and resell electronics that still have a useful life span as another way of keeping them out of the landfill.

We partner with local nonprofits to help support their mission in our local communities. A portion of the proceeds from the sale of refurbished items from our recycling program can be donated to a local non profit of your choice. It is part of our commitment to giving back to the communities that support our business. If this is a donation please select the organization you would like to support when scheduling your pickup.
